Perfect Grilled Cheese

Description

Oi this is the best grilled cheese you will ever have. Put some effort in, you grub.

Ingredients

  • White sourdough bread
  • 3 types of cheese. 1 with a stronger flavour, 1 that melts well, 1 with a milder flavour. A good pairing example is Pecorino, Swiss, Cheddar.
  • Basil pesto
  • Mayonnaise
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Select two slices of sourdough with consistent shape, not too many big holes.
  2. Pre-heat a pan on a medium-low heat.
  3. Coat the outer sides of the sourdough with mayonnaise. This will result in a much more crispy grilled cheese.
  4. Coat the inside of one piece of sourdough with pesto.
  5. Cut thin slices of the cheeses you've selected. Ensure you can get a full layer of each but bear in mind things like how strong the stronger cheese is and maybe have a little less if you want to achieve good balance.
  6. Place the pesto coated sourdough in the pan, mayonnaise side down. Layer your cheese on top in even layers. Place the second sourdough slice on top, mayonnaise side out.
  7. Wait a few minutes and use a spatula to check the colour of the toasting side. Once it's achieved a golden brown, flip it.
  8. Wait for the same to happen on the other side, by the time this happens the cheese should have melted nicely.
  9. Take your grilled cheese out, cut it diagonally because you're sophisticated, and sprinkle a pinch of salt over the top. Serve.
